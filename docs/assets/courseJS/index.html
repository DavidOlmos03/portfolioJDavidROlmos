First steps

I'm Juan David Ruiz Olmos. I was born into a humble family in Pitalito, Huila. When I was 2 years old, my family moved to Puerto Rico, Caquetá, where I grew up. I studied until 9th grade in this town, but later my family relocated to Florencia, the capital of Caquetá, as my sister got into university. After that, we moved to Medellín.

Career

When I finished high school, I was uncertain about which career to pursue. I had an interest in medicine, but studying this field at a public university proved to be quite challenging. Consequently, I decided to start my degree in mathematics, a choice that, in retrospect, I believe was one of the best decisions of my life. This path ignited a deep love for knowledge and personal growth, as I came to appreciate the complexities of life and science in general.


Last years

Two years later, I made another important decision and started my second degree in systems engineering. Driven by my passion for knowledge, I decided to leave behind my studies in mathematics and embark on this new journey in physics.
